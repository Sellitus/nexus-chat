Nexus Chat Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

Nexus Chat is designed with privacy as a core principle. All AI processing happens locally on your device using on-device language models. We do not collect, transmit, or store your conversations on any external servers.

Data Collection

We do not collect:

  • Your conversations or chat history
  • Personal information
  • Usage analytics or tracking data

Camera Permission

Nexus Chat requests camera access to enable vision-language model features, allowing you to share images with the AI for analysis. Images are processed entirely on your device and are never uploaded to external servers.

Microphone Permission

If enabled, voice input is processed on-device for speech-to-text conversion. Audio data is not transmitted externally.

Internet Access

Internet permission is used for:

  • Downloading AI models from Hugging Face (optional, user-initiated)
  • Web search functionality (optional, when enabled by user)
  • Google Drive backup/restore (optional, requires user sign-in)
  • Premium subscription verification via Google Play

Local Storage

All data including conversation history, settings, and downloaded models are stored locally on your device. You have full control to delete this data at any time through the app or by uninstalling.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Play Billing: Used for premium subscriptions. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Drive: Optional backup feature. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • DuckDuckGo/Brave Search: Optional web search. Queries are sent to the selected search provider when web search is enabled.

Children's Privacy

Nexus Chat is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact
